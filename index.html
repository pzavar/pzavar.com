Grubmatch: Restaurant Decision Made Fun & Fair

Project Overview

GrubMatch solves the age-old problem of "Where should we eat?" by providing a gamified, Tinder-like swiping interface for restaurant selection that generates matches based on the preferences of all group members. This mobile app helps friends and couples quickly find restaurants they'll all enjoy!

Simply create a group, copy the 5-digit code to share with your swipe partners, and begin swiping asynchronously on restaurants within your input range. As soon as everyone in the group swipes right on the same restaurant, there's a Match, and the session comes to an end.

This app was built as a fun side project while in grad school, but I also cultivated my skills in product management, prompt engineering, UX/UI design, and native app development.

EngageAI: Transforming Classroom Engagement

Project Overview

My team and I built an AI-powered education tool that allows instructors to upload recorded online Zoom lectures and receive an intuitive and actionable engagement analytics dashboard, helping pinpoint where students are most engaged (or lost). It is built using a custom-trained computer vision architecture (ResNet-50) that maps facial emotions to engagement levels.

The app is secure, self-contained, and does not expose our API. We do not store any student info, and after each session the lecture video that is uploaded by an instructor and any analysis generated using the video is wiped from our system, leaving us safe from most cyber attacks.

EngageAI System Overview

My Role

  • Owned product management by identifying key market differentiators and crafting a compelling value proposition that addressed critical pain points in virtual education
  • Developed a robust React-based analytics dashboard that visualized complex engagement metrics through intuitive data visualizations and instructor-focused UX
  • Collaborated with backend engineer, Ephrem Tilahun, to ensure seamless integration of the ML-powered engagement analysis backend with frontend visualization components
  • Led the technical demonstration to industry judges, showcasing the platform's capabilities and real-time analytics features and ultimately winning the Best Tech award between our MSIS cohort.
