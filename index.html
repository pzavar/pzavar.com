From Iran to Dubai to the United States, my journey across continents has shaped me into a leader with a truly global perspective. After graduating from UCLA with my Computer Science degree, I joined FAST Enterprises and was quickly assigned to the Iowa Department of Revenue Modernization Program. There, I rapidly earned such trust from both clients and leadership that I was promoted to manage the team responsible for govconnect.iowa.gov—a role typically reserved for those with years more experience. This unexpected acceleration revealed what continues to drive me today: finding elegant technical solutions to complex human problems.

I thrive at the intersection of product development and stakeholder collaboration. My technical foundation in programming, AI, and data science enables me to lead end-to-end software implementations while skillfully consulting with clients to translate complex requirements into practical solutions. As the focal point between technical teams, executives, and government stakeholders, I orchestrated the evolution of Iowa's taxpayer portal that now serves 1.5 million citizens annually. My implemented features reduced processing times by days and improved user experience enough to slash abandonment rates by over 20%—tangible evidence of my ability to deliver business value through technology.

What drives me is identifying the exact point where technical capability meets human need. Whether I'm conducting user interviews, rebuilding legacy systems, or designing new experiences, I excel at translating complex requirements into elegant solutions that delight both stakeholders and users.

As I complete my MS in Information Systems at UW in 2025, I'm seeking to apply this unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen as a Product Manager or Solutions Engineer where I can continue bridging gaps and building transformative products.
