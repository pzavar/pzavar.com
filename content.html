My Reading List

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman

A groundbreaking exploration of the two systems that drive the way we think and make decisions.

Zero to One

Peter Thiel

Notes on startups, or how to build the future by the co-founder of PayPal.

Inspired

Marty Cagan

How to create tech products customers love. A product management classic.

Hooked

Nir Eyal

How to build habit-forming products that keep users coming back.

The Lean Startup

Eric Ries

A methodology for developing businesses and products that shortens product development cycles.

Cracking the PM Interview

Gayle Laakmann McDowell

How to land a product manager job in a tech company.

The Design of Everyday Things

Don Norman

A powerful primer on how and why some products satisfy customers while others frustrate them.

Measure What Matters

John Doerr

How Google, Bono, and the Gates Foundation rock the world with OKRs.
